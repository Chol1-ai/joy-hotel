Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room at Joy Hotel?

Head to our Book Your Stay page, choose a room, pick your dates and submit the reservation form. You'll receive a confirmation code instantly.

Can I manage or cancel an existing reservation?

Yes. Go to the "Manage My Booking" tab on the Booking page and enter your confirmation code and email to view or cancel your reservation.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be requested via the special requests field when booking.

How quickly will I hear back after contacting you?

Our team typically responds to messages sent through this page within 24 hours.
